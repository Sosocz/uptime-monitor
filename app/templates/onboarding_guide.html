Create Your First Monitor

Monitors check your websites and APIs every minute to ensure they're online. When something goes down, you'll be notified immediately.

How to create a monitor:

  1. Click "Create monitor" button on the dashboard
  2. Enter your website name and URL
  3. Choose check interval (60 seconds recommended)
  4. Click "Create Monitor"
